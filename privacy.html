Privacy Policy

Privacy Built Into SafeScroll.

SafeScroll is built around one simple principle: you control your feed—not your data. We process only the information necessary to synchronize your modes, personalize filtering and protect your account.

🛡️

Secure. Private. AI-powered.

Minimum necessary data, transparent controls and revocable device access.

How we protect your data

Designed with practical safeguards.

🔐

Secure Authentication

Hashed passwords, protected sessions, reset flows and future two-factor support.

🧠

AI Processing

Metadata, OCR and visible-frame analysis are used only for filtering decisions.

👤

Private Preferences

Your modes and learning data belong to your account and can be edited or deleted.

💻

Connected Devices

Review, revoke and disconnect browser sessions whenever you choose.

What we store

Only what the product needs.

1
Account details

Name, email, password hash and account status.

2
Modes and preferences

Preferred topics, blocked categories, schedules and safety rules.

3
Connected devices

Device labels, session identifiers and last-sync information.

4
Filtering feedback

Optional relevant/irrelevant feedback used to improve each mode.

Frame storage policy

Visible video frames are intended to be processed temporarily for classification and not stored permanently.

Privacy Promise

SafeScroll exists to give users more control over what they watch—not to collect more information about them.

We aim to process the minimum amount of data necessary to deliver personalization, synchronization and safety controls.
