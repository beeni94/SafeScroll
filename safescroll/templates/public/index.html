FAQ

Common questions

Does SafeScroll replace every Short?

No. Your native feed stays primary. SafeScroll filters only content with positive unwanted-content evidence.

Can I create different modes?

Yes. Build Study, Child, Fun, Islamic, or fully custom modes and switch from the extension.

Will relevant Shorts be replaced?

Relevant Shorts are locked after the final decision so late responses cannot replace them.

Does Child Mode prevent extension removal?

It can protect mode switching with a PIN, but Chrome users can still disable extensions in browser settings.
