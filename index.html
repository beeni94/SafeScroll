FAQ

Common questions

Does SafeScroll replace every Short?

No. The native feed stays primary. SafeScroll only filters content with positive unwanted-content evidence.

Can I create different modes?

Yes. You can build Study, Child, Fun, Islamic or fully custom modes and switch them from the extension.

Will relevant Shorts be replaced?

Relevant Shorts are locked after the final decision so late backend responses cannot replace them.

Does Child Mode prevent extension removal?

It can protect mode switching with a PIN, but normal Chrome users can still disable extensions from browser settings.
